Week 4 Preparation Guide

Reading

Required: Textbook Chapter 8 and Chapter 9. In Chapter 9 there are several examples that won’t be covered in lecture. The examples about Gambler’s Ruin are optional but please read the others.

Highly recommended: Pitman Sections 3.2, 6.2

Practice Problems

Pitman x.y.z means Exercise z of Section x.y and x.rev.z means Exercise z of the Review Exercises at the end of Chapter x.

  • Pitman 6.rev.21, 6.rev.24a
